## Ingredients

- 2 cups rice
- ~ 2 cups water

## Instructions

1. Wash rice
2. Fill with water until one knuckle depth.
3. Bring to simmer with lid off.
4. Once boiling, turn down to low and cover with lid.
5. Cook for 13 minutes.
6. Remove from stove, rest for 10 minutes (semi-optional).
7. Fluff and serve.
